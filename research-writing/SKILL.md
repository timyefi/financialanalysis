# Research Writing Skill

This skill standardizes fixed-income research writing for BondClaw.

## Purpose

- Produce research notes that are conclusion-first, evidence-driven, and easy to scan.
- Keep tone calm, direct, and professional.
- Support macro, rates, credit, convertibles, multi-asset, fund, and trading workflows.

## Style Rules

- Start with the judgment, then explain the mechanism, then give evidence.
- Prefer short sections, clear headings, and concrete data.
- Always tie conclusions back to source evidence or explicit assumptions.
- Do not include personal names in prompts, examples, or fallback copy.

## Typical Output Structure

1. Core conclusion
2. Main mechanism
3. Evidence and numbers
4. Operational implications
5. Risks and caveats
